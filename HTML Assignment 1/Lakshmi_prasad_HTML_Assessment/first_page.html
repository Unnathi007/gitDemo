Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India. It is the most populous city in the India,
with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, Delhi has been a major settlement for two millennia,
its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
